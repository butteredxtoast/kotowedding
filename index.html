Where to Stay

We’re especially excited to share our city with the folks coming in from out of town! As you start packing, keep in mind that San Francisco weather is unique and covered in microclimates. Anything is possible in February, it could be a beautiful, sunny 70 degree day, it could be cold and densely foggy, it could rain (we’re really hoping it doesn’t rain) and no matter what it will definitely be windy. The weather could actually be all of those in the span of one day, or the span of a couple miles, honestly. Layers are your friends.

If you’re wondering where to stay, you can probably find a great Airbnb somewhere in the Mission, which would leave you close to the Spacement. Also, neighborhoods like Hayes Valley, Panhandle, Noe Valley, and Inner Sunset are all really cute. If a hotel is more your jam, most of them are located around Civic Center and are easily accessible from SFO by public transit. Getting around San Francisco on public transportation is fairly easy, we recommend downloading the Clipper app which you can use on MUNI and BART, and if you’re up for going up the occasional hill we recommend walking as much as you can. The city is gorgeous, and the entire peninsula is only 7x7 miles, so your next destination may be closer than you think!
